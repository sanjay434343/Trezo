Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 22, 2026

Welcome to Trezo. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Trezo application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

When you use Trezo, the way we handle your data is strictly split between cloud and local storage:

  • Cloud Data (Account Information): To provide seamless authentication, we only collect and store your name and email address securely in the cloud.
  • Offline Data (Asset Information): All the information you input regarding your personal assets (including names, serial numbers, purchase dates, documents, and images) is stored strictly offline on your device. None of your asset data is uploaded, synced, or stored on any cloud servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device-specific crash logs to improve app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

Trezo uses your limited cloud information strictly to provide authentication:

  • Your email and name are used solely to verify your identity and secure your app entry.
  • All push notifications and reminders regarding expiring warranties or custom dates are generated locally on your device without leaving it.

3. Data Security and Offline Privacy

We built Trezo with a privacy-first approach. Because your asset data never leaves your device, it is inherently protected from cloud breaches. The only data we store in the cloud (your name and email) is protected by industry-standard authentication protocols.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties. We may use trusted third-party services (like Firebase) solely for the purpose of authentication and secure data hosting.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You may delete individual assets or your entire account at any time directly from within the App. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@trezo.app.
